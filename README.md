# Wienerberger AG - Integrated Three-Statement Model

A fully linked three-statement financial model of Wienerberger AG
(Vienna Stock Exchange: WIE), the European building-materials group,
built in Excel.

## Summary
- **Statements:** Fully integrated Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and
  Cash Flow Statement
- **Historicals:** 5 years (2021-2025) from reported consolidated accounts
- **Projections:** Forward years driven by a small set of operating assumptions
- **Key feature:** Statements are fully linked - the balance sheet balances
  automatically with no plugs

## Approach
- Revenue is the master driver; cost lines projected as % of revenue at
  normalised, mid-cycle levels (reflecting the company's cyclical 2021–2025 range)
- Working capital driven by days (DSO / DIO / DPO)
- PP&E roll-forward (opening + capex - D&A - disposals)
- Debt schedule with average-debt interest (iterative calculation enabled)
- Cash flow statement links to all schedules; ending cash ties to the
  balance sheet cash line

## Supporting Schedules
- Working capital
- PP&E roll-forward
- Debt schedule and interest
